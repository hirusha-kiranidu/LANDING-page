How It Works

A simple flow designed for real-world child safety

  1. 01

    Child wears the smart device

    The wearable stays comfortable all day while collecting location and environmental safety data.

  2. 02

    Device sends data to KidCloud

    IoT connectivity securely transfers updates to the KidCloud cloud system in real time.

  3. 03

    Parents monitor updates in the app

    Parents view movement history, status updates, and live notifications from anywhere.

  4. 04

    AI detects risk and sends alerts

    KidCloud AI detects unusual behavior and triggers instant safety alerts for faster decisions.
